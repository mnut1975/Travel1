Japan


Japan is a country littered with culture, from climbing the sacred Mount Fuji to the street food markets of Osaka. Japan has a wealth of history from Fuedal Japan and the times of the Samurai, Coming into the modern day with the futuristic Bullet Train. And if you fancy a bit of winter sports, Japan has some fo the best Ski resorts in the world. Dont forget to try the hot springs at Onsen baths that have been present since at least the 8th Century
